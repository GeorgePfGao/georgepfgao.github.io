About

Dr Pengfei Gao is a Lecturer in Finance at the School of Management, Swansea University. Before joining Swansea, he was a University Teacher in Accounting and Finance at Loughborough Business School.

He received his PhD in Accounting and Finance from Cardiff Business School in 2024, following an MSc in Social Science Research Methods and an MSc in Accounting and Finance from Cardiff Business School.

His research focuses on FinTech, corporate disclosure, corporate innovation, and sustainable finance, with a particular interest in textual analysis, machine learning, and the narratives behind financial figures and corporate behaviour.

Research Architecture

Financial Technology

Emerging technologies, digital assets, market narratives, disclosure hype, and the valuation consequences of technological attention.

Corporate Information

Textual disclosure, audit penalties, local-government resonance, and the informational channels that shape firm risk and investor response.

Sustainable Finance

Corporate green innovation, carbon emissions, global value chains, ESG performance, and the financial incentives behind sustainability decisions.
